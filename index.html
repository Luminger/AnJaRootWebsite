AnJaRoot

Android Java Root for Android >= 2.3

AnJaRoot stands for Android Java Root, and it's just that - a replacement for the previous generation of supersuer access on Android. The days of calling su to execute scripts in a limited environment are over, developers are now able to perform previously restricted actions directly from Java!

Don't forget to visit the AnJaRoot XDA Thread if you have questions, problems or you just want to leave a comment. Ideas, patches and pull requests are also welcome at any time!

You may also consider supporting the development of AnJaRoot by getting a copy of AnJaRoot Donate.
